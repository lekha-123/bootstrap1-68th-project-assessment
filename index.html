Hi!

eeping in mind the thoughts of the patriotic monk Swami Vivekananda, a group of persons started a Vidyalaya in His name in the year 1980 on 19th october. Being an auspicious day for Vidyarambha Vijayadasami day – ‘Sri Vivekananda Vidyalaya’ seed was sown by Poojaneeya Swami Kumaranandaji Maharaj 0f Sri Ramakrishna Mutt, Natrampalli. It has been gradually grown and now we have a full fledged campus of SRI VIVEKANANDA MATRIC. HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL at Devalapuram. We have also planned to start a eSSE stream from the academic year 2016-17 at 17, Eswarachari Street, Ambur.
